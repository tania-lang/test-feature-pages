What you get
  • Record it.
    A Chrome extension that captures your screen, browser, or any tab. It tracks your clicks and actions automatically.
  • Edit it.
    Change your script like editing a doc. Trupeer re-syncs the video to match. No re-recording.
  • Voice it.
    AI replaces your raw voiceover with a polished version. 100+ accents and styles. Removes filler words.
